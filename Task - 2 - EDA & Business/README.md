# 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Business Intelligence – Task 2

## 🎯 Objective
To uncover patterns, trends, and relationships within the dataset and develop proficiency in SQL for data extraction and business intelligence reporting.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Matplotlib & Seaborn (Visualization)
- SQL (Business Queries)
- Jupyter Notebook
- PowerPoint / Excel (Dashboard Mock-up)

---

## 📌 Task Implementation

### 1️⃣ Descriptive Statistics & Univariate Analysis
- Calculated summary statistics using `.describe()`
- Checked missing values
- Created histograms and bar charts
- Analyzed distribution of numerical & categorical variables

### 2️⃣ SQL for Business Questions
Business questions solved using SQL:
- Top 5 products by revenue
- Monthly revenue trend
- Top performing region
- Customer acquisition trend
- Average order value
- Revenue by product category

### 3️⃣ Multivariate Analysis & Correlation
- Created scatter plots
- Generated heatmap for correlation
- Analyzed relationship between marketing spend and revenue

### 4️⃣ Static Dashboard Mock-up
Key KPIs Proposed:
- Total Revenue
- Monthly Growth
- Average Order Value
- Top Products
- Customer Growth Rate
- Region-wise Sales

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Marketing spend positively impacts revenue
- Certain product categories contribute highest sales
- Revenue shows monthly growth trend
- Customer acquisition varies by season
