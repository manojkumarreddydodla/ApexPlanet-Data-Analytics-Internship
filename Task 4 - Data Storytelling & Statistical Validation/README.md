## 📊 Business Problem

Customer retention declines after the first purchase,  
impacting long-term revenue growth.

We investigated whether repeat customers contribute significantly more revenue than one-time customers.

---

## 🔬 Hypothesis Testing

### Null Hypothesis (H₀):
There is no difference in spending between repeat and one-time customers.

### Alternative Hypothesis (H₁):
Repeat customers spend significantly more than one-time customers.

### Statistical Test Used:
Two-Sample T-Test
